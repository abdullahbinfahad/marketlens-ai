📌 Project Overview

MarketLens AI is an AI‑powered decision‑support system built for the Silk Road e‑commerce ecosystem. It addresses core pain points in cross‑border trade: intelligent product selection, real‑time overseas market analysis, multi‑lingual customer service, supply chain risk forecasting, and automated compliance. By fusing large language models, predictive analytics, and a dynamic weighted scoring engine, we deliver a one‑stop smart assistant that empowers Jiangsu exporters to go global with confidence.

⚡ Core Features

Trend Intelligence

Real‑time demand signals from Google Trends & cross‑border platforms, with 30‑day forecasting.

AI Sentiment Mining

LLM‑powered review analysis to uncover market gaps and competitor weaknesses.

Dynamic Scoring Engine

Weighted multi‑factor model (demand, profit, risk, competition) auto‑tuned for each market.

Risk Matrix

Monte Carlo simulations for tariff, logistics, and currency volatility.

Interactive Dashboard

Streamlit‑powered cockpit with 3D visualizations, dynamic gauges, and exportable reports.

Multi‑language Ready

Built‑in multi‑lingual AI customer service for seamless global communication.
