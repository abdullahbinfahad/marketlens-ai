MarketLens AI

OPC Intelligent Cross‑Border Product Assistant

📌 Project Overview

MarketLens AI is a next‑generation OPC (AI‑Agent) intelligent decision‑support system purpose‑built for Silk Road cross‑border e‑commerce. It tackles the hardest problems in global trade: what to sell, where to sell it, and how to win. By fusing LLM‑powered review mining, real‑time trend forecasting, a dynamic weighted scoring engine, and Monte‑Carlo risk simulation, MarketLens AI delivers a single, trustworthy Opportunity Score — so Jiangsu exporters can launch with confidence on the global stage.

⚡ Core Features

📊

Trend Intelligence

Real‑time demand signals from Google Trends & cross‑border platforms with 30‑day forecasting.

🧠

AI Sentiment Mining

LLM review analysis to uncover market gaps, unmet needs, and competitor weaknesses.

⚖️

Dynamic Scoring Engine

Bayesian‑optimized weights for demand, profit, risk & competition — auto‑tuned per market.

🛡️

Risk Matrix 2.0

Monte‑Carlo simulations for tariff shocks, logistics delays, and currency volatility.

📈

Interactive Dashboard

Streamlit cockpit with 3D charts, dynamic gauges, heatmaps, and exportable reports.

🌍

Multi‑Language Ready

Built‑in multilingual AI customer service for seamless global communication.
