MarketLens AI

Smart Cross‑Border Product Intelligence

What We Build

MarketLens AI is an AI‑powered decision agent designed for the realities of Silk Road e‑commerce. It solves the single biggest question facing cross‑border sellers today: what product will succeed in which market? By combining real‑time trend data, LLM‑driven review analysis, and a dynamic scoring engine, MarketLens turns uncertainty into a clear, ranked opportunity. It’s not just a tool — it’s a co‑pilot for global trade, built to help Jiangsu exporters move faster, smarter, and with confidence.

Core Capabilities

MarketLens AI integrates real‑time trend intelligence that forecasts demand signals across cross‑border platforms, AI‑powered sentiment mining that reads customer reviews to spot market gaps and competitor weaknesses, a dynamic scoring engine that balances demand, profit, risk, and competition using adaptive weights, a risk matrix that simulates tariff changes, shipping disruptions, and currency shifts, an interactive dashboard with 3D visualizations and live reports, and built‑in multi‑language AI assistance that helps sellers communicate with global audiences. Together, these capabilities create a complete intelligence backbone for modern Silk Road e‑commerce.
