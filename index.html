MarketLens AI

Smart Cross‑Border Product Intelligence

What We Build

MarketLens AI is an intelligent decision-making agent built specifically for Silk Road cross-border e-commerce. It answers the most critical question every international seller faces: which products will perform best in targeted overseas markets? Leveraging real-time market trend insights, LLM-based consumer review analysis — supported by advanced AI frameworks including DeepSeek and a proprietary dynamic evaluation system, MarketLens cuts through market uncertainty to deliver clear, prioritized business opportunities. More than just a regular analytics tool, it acts as a dedicated strategic co-pilot for global trade. Built to empower global and chinese exporters, it enables businesses to expand faster, operate with sharper market awareness, and make data-backed decisions with full confidence.

核心能力

MarketLens AI 集成了 实时趋势情报，预测跨境平台的需求信号； AI 评论挖掘，洞察市场缺口与竞争对手弱点； 动态评分引擎，平衡需求、利润、风险和竞争权重； 风险矩阵，模拟关税变化、物流中断与汇率波动； 交互式仪表板，提供 3D 可视化与实时报告； 以及内建的 多语言 AI 助手，帮助卖家与全球受众无缝沟通。 这些能力共同构成现代丝路电商的完整情报中枢。
